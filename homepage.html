Who We Are

Rural Great Minds (RGM) is an initiative that aims at promoting education in rural Ugandan schools, mainly primary schools. This is achieved mainly through awarding scholarships to students based on excellence in academics, sports, hygiene, discipline, confidence and leadership.

Many talented pupils fail to obtain a good education, even at the elementary-school level, just because their parents cannot afford to pay for it. Even some of the talented ones that can afford it are not recognized for their achievements and, so, lack inspiration. Students in rural areas often lack opportunities that can boost their confidence and innate creativity, due to limited resources. RGM comes in to support such pupils, to teach them from a young age that good efforts of any form can pay off.

How RGM Started

RGM founder Annet has a big passion for cancer research, a field in which she is actively engaged. She is equally passionate about promoting quality education, especially in village schools, like the one she studied in as a child, in rural Uganda. In 2020, she organised a successful fundraising for Kabasumba Primary School, where she happened to visit in 2019 and observed conditions that inspired her to lend a helping hand.

With her friends, workmates at McDonald’s in Japan, and professors at the University of Tsukuba (where Annet was studying as an undergraduate), together they raised 63,500 yen (equal to about 635 US dollars at the time), which was used to improve the condition of the school (Pictures 1 and 2).

Our Goal

To promote education, talents and life skills, including confidence, self-esteem, leadership, and social interaction among rural pupils

Moments with the RGM Scholars

Beyond Scholarships

Even though everyone is talented in their own way, and we try to reward as many great talents as possible, not every pupil gets an award. For that reason, we also engage in activities that can make rural schools more enjoyable, memorable and impactful to their pupils. We contribute in the following ways:

01

Planting Trees

Donate various fruit trees that are taken care of by pupils, teaching them about environmental conservation and responsibility while providing sustainable resources for the school community.

02

Career Guidance

Offer comprehensive career guidance sessions to various schools, helping students understand their potential paths and inspiring them to pursue their dreams through education.

03

Sports Equipment

Provide essential sports equipment like balls and nets, promoting physical activity, team building, and healthy competition among students.

04

Hygiene Facilities

Install and maintain proper hygiene facilities, including handwashing stations and water tanks, ensuring a healthy learning environment for all students.
